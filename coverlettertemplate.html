Cover Letter Template

The cover letter is a very important step in your Career Journey. Your cover letter is not a repeat of your resume. It is a quick synopsis of what you can bring to the table. The following illustrations will serve as guidance for how to format your cover letter. The cover letter here is broken down into three parts (the heading, the Body, and the Closing). There are also links to where you can build a proper cover letter on indeed according to the positions you are looking for.




Heading: This includes the Name, Address, Contact Information, Date, Recipient Name and Address



Body: This includes the Greeting, the Body and the Closing.
